## Overview

Havoc Demon Hunter is a melee DPS spec built around demon form uptime. Everything in the kit feeds into and benefits from Demonic windows triggered by Eye Beam, with Metamorphosis serving as the major burst cooldown that resets Eye Beam and Blade Dance on cast via Chaotic Transformation. Fury generation is primarily passive through Demon Blades (baseline in Midnight) and Soul Fragment pickup. Between cooldown windows, the spec spends Fury on Chaos Strike and Blade Dance filler. Gameplay is high-mobility but demands precise sequencing during burst windows.

---

## Role in Raid

- **Primary role:** Melee DPS
- **Raid debuff:** Chaos Brand (SpellID: 255260) — applies a 5% magic damage taken increase to the target. Maintained automatically. Any Demon Hunter (any spec) provides this; only one is needed per group.
- **Group defensive:** Darkness (SpellID: 196718) — 20% chance to absorb a lethal hit for all players inside the area. RNG-dependent; not a guaranteed save.
- **Utility coverage:** Interrupt (Disrupt), AoE stun (Chaos Nova + Focused Ire), hard CC (Imprison, Sigil of Misery), self-dispel (conditional talent), movement tools.
- **Notable mobility:** Among the best self-repositioning in melee. Fel Rush (2 charges), Vengeful Retreat, Double Jump, and Glide provide sustained movement capability across an entire encounter.

## Rotation / Priority

### Core Mechanic Understanding

Havoc's rotation is a priority system around **demon form windows**, not a fixed cast loop. Two types of demon form exist:

- **Demonic window:** Triggered by Eye Beam. Lasts 5 seconds base, extended by Fury spending via Shattered Destiny. Occurs approximately every 20 seconds via Cycle of Hatred.
- **Metamorphosis:** 3-minute cooldown. On cast, resets Eye Beam and Blade Dance (Chaotic Transformation), immediately enabling another Eye Beam for a back-to-back Demonic window.

During demon form: Chaos Strike becomes Annihilation, Blade Dance becomes Death Sweep. Both deal significantly more damage. Fury spending extends the window via Shattered Destiny.

**Inertia (Mover builds):** Felblade or Fel Rush must be used immediately before Fury spending during burst windows to maintain the 15% damage buff. This is why Inertia openers include explicit Felblade casts before Essence Break or Death Sweep.

**Fury generation in Midnight:** Felblade generates only 15 Fury (down from 40 in The War Within). Overall Fury generation is slower and more passive. Fury capping is less common; pooling Fury for burst windows is more deliberate.

## Defensives

> **RaidLens usage:** Use this section to assess whether a player had a defensive available during a high-damage window and whether they deployed it correctly.

### Blur
- **SpellID:** 198589
- **Effect:** Reduces all damage taken by 35% for 10 seconds. Dodge component removed in Midnight. With Desperate Instincts (SpellID: 205411), also provides 10% passive DR below 35% health.
- **Charges:** 2 (second charge from Demonic Resilience, SpellID: 1266307 — always taken in current builds)
- **Cooldown:** 1 minute per charge
- **GCD:** Yes
- **Usage:** Not a panic button. Use proactively before a planned damage event. Two charges on a 1-minute recharge allows deployment twice in a 2-minute window.

### Desperate Instincts
- **SpellID:** 205411
- **Effect:** Passive. Blur's damage reduction increased by an additional 10% (total: 35%). Also provides 10% passive DR when below 35% health — including on hits that would push the player below that threshold.
- **Always talented** in current builds.

### Darkness
- **SpellID:** 196718
- **Effect:** Area grants all players inside a 20% chance to absorb a lethal hit. Lasts 8 seconds.
- **Cooldown:** 3 minutes with Pitch Black (SpellID: 389783) — always taken. Base: 5 minutes.
- **GCD:** Yes
- **Caveats:** 20% is not a guarantee. Do not plan raid cooldown assignments around Darkness as a reliable save. Players must be inside the area.

### Deflecting Dance
- **SpellID:** 427901
- **Effect:** Grants an absorb shield during Death Sweep and Blade Dance casts, absorbing up to 15% of max health. Very brief. High skill-ceiling — used to outplay one-shot mechanics by timing a cast to coincide with a dangerous hit.

### Demon Muzzle
- **SpellID:** 1266329
- **Effect:** Interrupting with Disrupt (SpellID: 183752) reduces magic damage taken by 15% for 12 seconds.
- **Cooldown:** Tied to Disrupt (15s). Off-GCD.
- **Caveats:** Only valuable when interruptible casts exist. Strong in Mythic+; situational in raid.

### Soul Rending
- **SpellID:** 204909
- **Effect:** Passive. 10% Leech always; higher in demon form. Sustained passive self-healing throughout a fight.

### First In, Last Out
- **SpellID:** 1266497
- **Effect:** Fel Rush grants a small decaying absorb shield. Incidental defensive value; not a planned cooldown.
